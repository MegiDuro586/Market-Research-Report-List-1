<p><p>Cardiac Rhythm Management (CRM) encompasses medical devices that regulate heart rhythms, primarily including pacemakers, defibrillators, and cardiac resynchronization therapy (CRT) devices. Pacemakers are used to manage bradycardia by delivering electrical impulses to maintain a regular heartbeat. Defibrillators, such as ICDs, are designed to detect and treat life-threatening arrhythmias by delivering shocks to restore normal heart rhythm. CRT devices synchronize heart contractions in patients with heart failure, improving efficiency and overall cardiac function. These innovations enhance patient outcomes through tailored rhythm management.</p></p>
