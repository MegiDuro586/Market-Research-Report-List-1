The 830 nm type is often used in biomedical applications, while the 1050 nm type is suited for short-range optical communications. The 1300 nm type is prevalent in telecommunications, offering reduced dispersion. The 1550 nm type is favored for long-distance fiber optic networks due to its low loss in standard optical fibers. Other markets may explore specialized or emerging applications.</p></p>
