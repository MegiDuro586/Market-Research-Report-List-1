<p><p>The Steam Turbine Generators market can be categorized into three main types: SVSS (Small Vacuum Steam Turbine Systems), SVMS (Small Vacuum Multi-stage Steam Turbine Systems), and MVMS (Medium Vacuum Multi-stage Steam Turbine Systems). SVSS are designed for smaller installations with lower steam pressure, ideal for small-scale energy production. SVMS offers more efficiency by utilizing multiple stages for steam expansion, making them suitable for varied industrial applications. MVMS, on the other hand, cater to medium-sized operations, providing a balance between performance and capacity.</p></p>
