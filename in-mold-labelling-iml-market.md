<p><p>In-Mold Labeling (IML) is a packaging process that integrates labels into the molding process for enhanced aesthetics and durability. The market features three primary types: Injection Molding IML, where labels are placed in the mold before injecting plastic; Blow Molding IML, suited for hollow containers like bottles; and Thermoforming IML, which involves heating sheets to conform to molds. Each process offers unique advantages, catering to different product requirements and manufacturing efficiencies, driving innovation in the packaging industry.</p></p>
