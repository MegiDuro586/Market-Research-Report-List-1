<p><p>Cancer ablation devices are specialized tools used to destroy cancer cells with minimal damage to surrounding tissues. Key market types include cryoablation, which freezes tumors; high-intensity focused ultrasound (HIFU), which uses focused sound waves; laser interstitial thermal therapy (LiTT), involving laser heat; microwave ablation (MWA), which employs microwave energy; and radiofrequency ablation (RFA), using radio waves. Additionally, irreversible electroporation (IRE) uses electric fields to disrupt cell membranes, while non-thermal ablation refers to methods that do not rely on heat.</p></p>
