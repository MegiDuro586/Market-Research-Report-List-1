<p><p>The PET film extrusion line market is segmented into multilayer and single layer types. Multilayer PET films are composed of multiple layers, enhancing barrier properties, mechanical strength, and versatility for various applications like packaging and electronics. Single layer PET films, on the other hand, consist of a single polymer layer, offering simpler manufacturing and cost-effectiveness for less demanding applications. Both types cater to different industry needs, with multilayer films often preferred for high-performance requirements, while single layer films serve basic functions efficiently.</p></p>
