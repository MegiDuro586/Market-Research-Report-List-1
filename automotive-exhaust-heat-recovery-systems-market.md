<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust Heat Recovery Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR)</li><li>Rankine Cycle Systems</li><li>Thermoelectric Generator</li><li>Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive exhaust heat recovery systems are designed to capture waste heat from exhaust gases to improve vehicle efficiency. Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) systems use heat exchangers to transfer thermal energy to the engine or other components. Rankine Cycle Systems convert heat into mechanical energy through fluid vaporization and condensation. Thermoelectric Generators utilize temperature differences to produce electrical energy. Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) integrates an electric motor-generator with a turbocharger to recover energy, enhancing performance and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
