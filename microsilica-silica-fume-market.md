<p><p>The microsilica market is categorized into three types: densified microsilica, semi-densified microsilica, and undecified microsilica. Densified microsilica features a compact form, enhancing its flowability and reducing dust, making it ideal for high-performance concrete. Semi-densified microsilica offers moderate density, balancing performance and handling ease. Undensified microsilica, composed of fine particles, is commonly used in applications requiring high pozzolanic activity but may be less convenient to handle. Each type caters to different industrial needs in construction and related sectors.</p></p>
