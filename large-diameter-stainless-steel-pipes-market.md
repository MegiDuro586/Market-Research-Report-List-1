<p><p>The large diameter stainless steel pipes market is primarily segmented into two types: welded and seamless stainless steel pipes. Welded stainless steel pipes are created by stitching together flat plates or strips, offering cost efficiency and versatility for various applications. Conversely, seamless stainless steel pipes are manufactured from a solid block of steel, resulting in higher strength and durability due to the absence of welds. Both types cater to different industrial needs, including construction, oil and gas, and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
