<p><p>The solventless resins market is categorized into three main types: single component, two components, and three components. Single component systems are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing, often providing ease of application. Two component resins involve a base and a hardener that must be mixed prior to use, enhancing performance characteristics. Three component systems add a third component, typically a filler or additive, to further tailor properties such as adhesion, flexibility, or curing speed, offering versatility for various applications.</p></p>
