<p><p>Industrial X-ray Computed Tomography (CT) encompasses two main market types: Line Beam Scanning and Cone Beam Scanning. Line Beam Scanning utilizes a linear X-ray source to capture images of objects, offering high-resolution data for analyzing complex geometries. In contrast, Cone Beam Scanning employs a cone-shaped X-ray beam, enabling rapid acquisition of volumetric data, ideal for larger components and three-dimensional imaging. Both techniques enhance non-destructive testing and quality control processes in various industries, such as manufacturing and aerospace.</p></p>
