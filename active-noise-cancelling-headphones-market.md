<p><p>The active noise-cancelling headphones market is categorized into three main types: over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear headphones. Over-ear headphones provide a larger ear cup that envelops the ear, offering superior noise isolation and comfort for extended use. On-ear headphones rest directly on the ears, balancing portability and sound quality. In-ear headphones, or earbuds, fit snugly inside the ear canal, providing excellent passive noise isolation, making them ideal for travel and daily commutes. Each type caters to different user preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
