<p><p>Multiple Orifice Valves (MOVs) are critical components in fluid control systems, available in manual and automatic types. Manual MOVs require operator intervention for regulation, offering simplicity and direct control, making them suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, automatic MOVs utilize actuators for remote or automated control, enhancing efficiency and precision in larger or complex systems. The choice between manual and automatic varies based on application needs, operational scale, and desired control level, impacting the overall MOV market dynamics.</p></p>
