<p><p>Nonfat Dry Milk (NFDM) is a powdered dairy product derived from skimmed milk, containing minimal fat content. The NFDM market is primarily categorized into two types: skimmed milk and whole milk. Skimmed milk powder is produced by removing fat, making it suitable for low-calorie diets and food formulations. Whole milk powder, on the other hand, retains its fat content, catering to higher nutritional needs and providing a creamier texture. Both types are essential in various food applications, including baking and confectionery.</p></p>
