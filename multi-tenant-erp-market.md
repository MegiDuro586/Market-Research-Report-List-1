<p><p>The multi-tenant ERP market can be divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based ERP systems operate on shared infrastructure, allowing multiple organizations to access the same application while benefiting from scalability, cost-effectiveness, and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises systems require individual installations on local servers, providing greater control and customization but potentially higher maintenance costs. Each market type has distinct advantages, catering to different organizational needs and preferences regarding flexibility, security, and resource management.</p></p>
