<p><p>The machine tool oils market includes various types essential for optimal machinery performance. Hydraulic oil is used to transfer power in hydraulic systems, ensuring smooth operation and minimizing wear. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, serves as a lubricant and coolant, providing effective protection against rust and corrosion. Sideway oil is specifically designed for slideways, offering superior adhesion and reduced friction, which enhances machine precision and longevity. Collectively, these oils play a vital role in enhancing machinery efficiency and lifespan.</p></p>
