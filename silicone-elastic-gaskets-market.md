<p><p>The silicone elastic gaskets market features various types, including saddle and waveform gaskets. Saddle gaskets are designed to provide a secure fit in applications where uneven surfaces are prevalent, ensuring a reliable seal. Waveform gaskets, on the other hand, utilize a wavy profile to enhance flexibility and compression, making them ideal for dynamic sealing environments. Both types are valued for their durability, temperature resistance, and ability to accommodate thermal expansion, catering to diverse industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics.</p></p>
